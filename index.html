Course Specification

Course Title: Data Visualization Design | Code: HCI 3401

A. General Information

Credit Hours: 4 Hours

Level: 3rd Year / 5th Level

Description: Focuses on designing effective data visualizations within HCI, leveraging design principles and cognitive psychology.

B. Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs)

Code Outcome Teaching Strategy
1.1Knowledge of visual perceptionDiscussions
2.1Use of visualization toolsLab Demonstrations
3.1Ethical judgment in designCase Studies

C. Course Topics

  1. Introduction to Data Visualization (6 hrs)
  2. Principles of Visual Perception (6 hrs)
  3. Designing with Data (12 hrs)
  4. Visualization Tools and Technologies (12 hrs)
  5. Storytelling with Data (6 hrs)
  6. Interaction and Animation (6 hrs)
  7. Evaluation and Testing (6 hrs)
  8. Accessibility in Design (6 hrs)
  9. Advanced Topics (12 hrs)
  10. Ethics in Data Visualization (6 hrs)
